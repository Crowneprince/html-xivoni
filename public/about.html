About Us

Company Background

Xivoni is an actuarial consulting firm formed in 2016. Xivoni is a registered service provider (RSP) by the Actuarial Society of South Africa (ASSA), registration number: RSP142/2019. Xivoni means mirror in Xitsonga. This word encompasses the idea of people working together to fulfil a common goal. We believe in walking with our clients to understand their problems, develop long lasting solutions and to communicate effectively so that the solutions are successfully implemented.

Our Journey

Since its establishment in 2016, Xivoni has assisted firms of various sizes in South Africa and in other African jurisdictions on an advisory basis to improve their product offerings, with regulatory compliance and establishing themselves in the market.

Xivoni’s partners are Actuarial Fellows in various jurisdictions (incl. South Africa, the UK, Kenya and Tanzania) and this enables the firm to stay close to developments in these markets. Over the last few years, Xivoni has continued to grow its staff complement in order to meet the increasing needs of its clients. Xivoni’s approach of walking the journey with its clients has assisted it to deliver exceptional quality due to its ability to focus its attention on the detail.

Recognising our journey thus far, Xivoni holds a strong position on accepting client engagements. We do not accept engagements that are beyond the scope of our abilities as this is counter to our values. Our agility and responsiveness to client needs is an aspect that we believe has been the cornerstone of our success and has allowed our firm to gradually develop mutual trust with our clients.
